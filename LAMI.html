LAMI

Leap (Motion Based) Audio Mixing interface

This project aimed at recreating a traditional mixing desk, which consists of knobs, faders and buttons, and replace them with a more visual based interface that can be more intuitively interacted with.

Colored spheres represent the audio tracks, and their position in the X and Z axis (left/right and back/forward) represent their pan and level parameters respectively. Users utilize a Leap Motion to grab and move these spheres around to manipulate the audio parameters.

This interface was published in the AES conference 2017. http://www.aes.org/e-lib/browse.cfm?elib=18661
