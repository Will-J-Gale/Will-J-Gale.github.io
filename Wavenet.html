Wavenet Pytorch

Diagram from the wavenet paper

Inspired by the following:

  • Wavenet
  • Real-Time Guitar Amplifier Emulation with Deep Learning
  • Neural Amp Modeller

This project aimed to extend the neural-amp-modeller to model the whole guitar signal chain (gate, drive, amp, cab, EQ) instead of just the amplifier and also focusing on high gain amps. Moreover it aimed to create a simple to understand pytorch version, as often commercial versions are highly abstracted and difficult to extract the actual architecutre of the model used. Similarly to the neural-amp-modeller a simple convnet architecutre was also tried for comparison.

All code can be found at Wavenet-Pytorch
